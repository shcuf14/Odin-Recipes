Pulled Pork

This pulled pork will blow your socks off.

Ingredients

  • Roughly 9 pound pork butt
  • Holy Gospel Rub
  • Half stick of butter
  • Half cup of light brown sugar
  • Optional: Honey Hog Hot rub

Steps

  1. Preheat smoker to 365
  2. Trim pork butt (Remove unwanted fat)
  3. Apply generous amount of Holy Gospel Rub and let sweat for 15-30 mins
  4. Put on smoker and spritz with applie cider vineagar every 2 hours. Leave until bark is desired color or until pork is around 165 interal. Should take 5-6 hours.
  5. Pull off smoker and put into pan. Add butter, sugar, and optional rub. Cover with foil and add back to smoker
  6. Leave for roughly another 3 hours until interal temp is 203-206
  7. Pull off and let rest for 30 mins. Pull pork and enjoy.
