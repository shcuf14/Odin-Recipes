Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground sausage
  • 2 pounds of Velveta chesse/li>
  • 4 Table spoons of Coffee Rub
  • 2 Cans of Rotell diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of shredded Guoda cheese
  • 1 can of cream of mushroom soup

Steps

  1. Preheat smoker to 375
  2. Brown sausage in frying pan or cast iron skillet
  3. Combine all ingredients into dutch oven
  4. Somke for 45 mins stiring every 15 mins
